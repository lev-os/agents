CONSTRAINTS

Ratchet Constraints

The seal that only tightens.

Ratchet Constraints

Admission gates that enforce invariants at the kernel level. Schema validation, term fences, and immutability checks.

If the kernel can't evaluate a policy, the action is denied. No inference, no repair, no silent failures.

ratchet-gate.yaml
gate: admission_control
mode: fail_closed

policy:
  - schema_valid: required
  - term_fence: enforced
  - capability_check: deny_unknown

on_fail: deny
class="code-comment"># no inference. no repair. no silent failures.
