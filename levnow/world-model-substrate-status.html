THE IDEA

What it is in one breath

Most AI agents are busy — they re-poll, re-embed, and re-reason on a clock whether or not anything changed. This inverts that. Every change to the world passes three gates, cheapest-first: is it legal? → did anything that matters actually change? → is it surprising enough to act on? A skip at any gate is ~free. Pass all three and the agent acts and seals a tamper-evident receipt.

The "surprise" judge is a swappable world-model the loop never trusts — feed it garbage and it safely re-renders. Cost tracks genuine novelty, not polling frequency.

Where the Constraint Manifold decides what may run, the World-Model Substrate decides what is worth running — and proves it.

THE MECHANISM

Three gates, cheapest first

A skip short-circuits the rest. Render only when all three pass.

G2 · Material?

THE HORIZON

The next 4 features — each ~2× the wow

replay → many minds → modeled minds → self-directing minds. They compound.

① Counterfactual Holodeck · 2×

Fork the receipt chain at any point, inject a what-if observation, replay forward deterministically. "What would it have believed if this fact were false?" → audit-by-counterfactual, synthetic training data, time-travel debugging.

② Shared-Mind Mesh · 4×

N agents reconcile against one content-addressed world-model. Supersede-CAS guarantees a single linear truth, no forks. A team of agents that provably share one mind — consensus by physics, not a database lock.

③ Avatars · 8×

Point the carrier at a person's output stream → a queryable, falsifiable model of them. Expresses uncertainty and contested beliefs (basin occupancy), not just plausible text. The only honest way to do an avatar: one you can interrogate.

④ Active Inference · 16×

Close the loop. The agent acts to minimize its expected future surprise — choosing what to poll, refresh, and ask, to learn fastest. Curiosity becomes a control law. Set-it-and-forget-it, made physical.
