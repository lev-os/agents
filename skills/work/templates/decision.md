# ADR: Decision Title

## How To Fill This Out

Use this template when a decision should outlive the session handoff. Decisions belong here when they are architectural, cross-workstream, policy-setting, or likely to be reused later.

Use uncertainty markers when needed:
- `[tbd]`
- `[unknown]`
- `[theory]`
- `[maybe: ..., confidence: ##%]`

Good:
- "This decision affects multiple future specs."
- "This decision came from a handoff and is worth promoting."

Bad:
- "This was just a one-off conversational preference."
- "This is a handoff note copied without context."
