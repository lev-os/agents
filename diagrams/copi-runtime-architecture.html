Missing Components

Audio Ingest Daemon
Nothing publishes to audio.segment. FileReplay adapter designed but unbuilt. PCM16 mono 16kHz spec locked.
Session Manager
FSM exists (13 states, 15 events, 11 passing tests) but is completely unwired from any daemon.
Callsign Filter
Needed between ASR and Intent to isolate own-aircraft transmissions. Currently processes all radio traffic.
Dead Streams

Created at startup, never published to or subscribed from. 3 of 8 streams are waste.
