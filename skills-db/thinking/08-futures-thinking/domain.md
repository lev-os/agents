
**Rationale:** Separate from general decision-making, this focuses specifically on thinking about and planning for the future across different time horizons.

- 10,000 Year Clock thinking - Civilization-scale timeframes
- Cathedral thinking - Multi-generational projects
- Long Bet Project - Accountable long-term predictions
- Six ways to think long-term (Roman Krznaric)

**Futures Methodologies:**
- IFTF Foresight-Insight-Action Cycle
- Generic Foresight Process (Voros)
- Framework Foresight (University of Houston)
- Delphi Method - Expert consensus building
- Futures Literacy Labs (UNESCO)
- Megatrends analysis
- Experiential futures

**Superforecasting (Extended):**

- Tournament-style prediction competitions
- Extremizing aggregated forecasts

- 2×2 matrix scenario development

**Strategic Foresight:**
- Identifying signals vs. noise
- Cone of uncertainty
