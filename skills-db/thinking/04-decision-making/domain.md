### Domain 4: **Decision-Making & Strategic Thinking** (85 frameworks)

**Rationale:** While Domain 2 focuses on avoiding errors (biases), this domain provides proactive frameworks for making better decisions and thinking strategically about competition, positioning, and long-term choices.

**Core content:**

**First Principles Reasoning:**
- Elon Musk's 3-step method, Aristotelian foundations
- Five Whys technique, Socratic questioning
- Cook vs. Chef mental model (reasoning vs. imitation)

**Probabilistic & Bayesian Thinking:**
- Annie Duke's "Thinking in Bets" framework
- Expected value calculation
- Confidence intervals and calibration
- Base rate analysis, outside view vs. inside view
- Bayes' theorem application and sequential updating

**Game Theory for Everyday Use:**
- Nash equilibrium, prisoner's dilemma
- Dominant strategy analysis, zero-sum vs. positive-sum
- Tit-for-tat, coordination games
- Mechanism design

**Strategic Frameworks:**
- Porter's Five Forces, SWOT, PESTLE analysis
- Blue Ocean Strategy, strategic group analysis
- BCG Growth-Share Matrix, value chain analysis
- VRIO analysis, McKinsey 7S, Ansoff Matrix

**Risk Assessment:**
- Risk matrices, Monte Carlo simulation
- Value at Risk (VaR), Expected Monetary Value
- Sensitivity analysis, scenario-based assessment

**Scenario Planning:**
- Shell method, 2×2 matrix approach
- Morphological analysis, horizon scanning
- Three Horizons framework, backcasting

**Superforecasting (Philip Tetlock):**
- 10 commandments for forecasters
- Brier score for accuracy measurement
- Fermi estimation, incremental belief updating
- Wisdom of crowds aggregation

**Sources:** Philip Tetlock, Annie Duke, Elon Musk, Michael Porter, Shell scenario planning, game theory literature

**Skills examples:** "Apply first principles to product innovation," "Use scenario planning for strategic uncertainty," "Calculate expected values for investment decisions," "Recognize game theory dynamics in competition," "Build superforecasting practices"

**Unique value:** Comprehensive decision toolkit from probability to strategy; includes cutting-edge forecasting research
