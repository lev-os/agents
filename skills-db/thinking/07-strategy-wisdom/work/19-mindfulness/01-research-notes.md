## Origin

- **Year**: 1979 (MBSR program founded)
- **Source**: Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction (MBSR), "Wherever You Go, There You Are"

## Core Concept
Mindfulness is awareness that arises from paying attention, on purpose, in the present moment, and nonjudgmentally. It's the practice of observing thoughts, sensations, and emotions without getting swept away by them. The formal practice includes body scan meditation, mindful yoga, and sitting meditation—each training the mind to return to present-moment awareness.
