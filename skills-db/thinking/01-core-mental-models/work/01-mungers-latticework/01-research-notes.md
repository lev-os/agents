# Research Notes: Munger's Latticework Approach

## Origin
- **Author**: Charlie Munger
- **Year**: 1990s-2000s (speeches and writings)
- **Source**: USC Business School speeches, Poor Charlie's Almanack, Berkshire Hathaway letters

## Core Concept
Munger's latticework of mental models is an interconnected framework where knowledge from diverse disciplines (psychology, economics, biology, physics, engineering) creates a robust decision-making system. Rather than viewing problems through a single disciplinary lens ("man with a hammer syndrome"), the latticework approach applies multiple mental models simultaneously to gain comprehensive understanding and avoid blind spots.
