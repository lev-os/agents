# Research Notes: Circle of Competence

## Origin
- **Authors**: Warren Buffett & Charlie Munger
- **Year**: Developed in 1990s-2000s investment practice
- **Source**: Berkshire Hathaway shareholder letters, public talks

## Core Concept
Circle of Competence is a mental model for limiting activities to areas where you have genuine expertise and understanding. The key insight is that knowing the boundaries of your expertise is more important than the size of that expertise. Buffett and Munger use a three-box framework: "In," "Out," and "Too hard" - explicitly acknowledging what you don't know.
